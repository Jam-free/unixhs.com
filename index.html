How to Play ABC Tiles

  • 1

    Find and match identical letters on the board to remove them.

  • 2

    Letters can only be matched if they can be connected by a line with no more than two turns.

  • 3

    Clear all letters from the board to complete each level.

  • 4

    Work quickly! The timer is running and you'll score higher for faster matches.
